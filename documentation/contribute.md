# Contribute

We’re excited to welcome contributions from the community to help grow and improve **Naira**! Your ideas, feedback, and code are invaluable in shaping a robust AI engineering platform for building enterprise-grade AI-native solutions. ✨🧠

Whether you’re fixing a bug, enhancing features, or suggesting improvements, we’d love to have you onboard.
To get started, check out our repositories. There, you’ll find all the guidelines and instructions to make your contribution seamless and impactful. 🛠️

### Ways You Can Contribute:
- Fix [bugs](https://github.com/search?q=org:naira-project%20is:issue%20is:open%20label:bug&type=issues) 🐞.
- Add new features 🌟.
- Improve [documentation](https://github.com/naira-project/naira-project.github.io) 📖.
- Suggest improvements by [creating an issue](https://github.com/naira-project/naira) 💡.

Let’s build the future of AI Engineering together! ❤️
